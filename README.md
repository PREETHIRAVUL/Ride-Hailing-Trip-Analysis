# Uber Ride-Hailing Trip Analysis

## Business Problem
Uber lacked centralized visibility into booking demand, revenue performance, trip efficiency, and customer payment behavior across locations and vehicle categories. This limited operational optimization, driver allocation, and revenue growth opportunities.

---

## Objective
Analyze ride-booking trends, operational efficiency, customer behavior, and revenue patterns to support data-driven business decisions.

## Key Analysis Performed

- Payment method analysis
- Trip distance and duration analysis
- Trip efficiency analysis
- Interactive dashboard creation using slicers, drill-throughs, and dynamic measures

---

## Key Insights
- Completed **103.7K bookings** generating approximately **$1.6M revenue**
- Average booking value reached **$15 per trip**
- Peak demand observed during **8–10 AM** and **6–9 PM**
- **UberX** contributed the highest booking volume and revenue
- Digital payments represented nearly **67%** of all transactions
- Most trips were short-distance urban rides
- Penn Station / Madison Square Garden was the highest-demand pickup location

---

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize driver allocation during peak demand hours
- Improve surge pricing strategies during high-demand periods
- Encourage digital payment adoption for operational efficiency
- Promote high-performing vehicle categories
- Improve ride distribution using trip distance and location insights
